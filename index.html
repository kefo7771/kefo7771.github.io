A little bit about me

As a new graduate of the Computer Science program at the University of Colorado Boulder, I am excited to apply what I learned to new ideas and projects. In truth, my journey into the land of programming has been short and incredibly eventful. Starting with learning alongside my brother for his high school programming class, I quickly fell in love with programming and enrolled back into CU to pursue a degree in Computer Science. After some hard work I am now on the other side of my second degree (first being in Political Science) and ready to take my first steps into the professional world. I hope that through this website you can learn a bit more about me and what I can offer.
